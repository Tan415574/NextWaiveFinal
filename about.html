How it started

We're both studying Information Systems at UCT and we kept noticing the same thing: local businesses around Cape Town with no website, no online presence, and no idea how much time they were wasting on manual processes.

We knew we could help. Between the two of us we had the technical skills and the business understanding to actually build something useful. So we started NextWaive.

What we do

We build websites, AI chatbots, social media systems, and e-commerce stores for small and medium businesses across South Africa. Everything is tailored to the client because no two businesses are the same.

We're not a massive agency. We're two people who care about doing good work, communicating honestly, and building things that genuinely help businesses grow.
